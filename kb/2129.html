--- title: "How do I access my organization account?" id: 2129 draftstate: inactive deleted: false source: https://success.docker.com/@api/deki/pages/2129/contents tags: - tag: "article:nontechnical" - tag: "stage:reviewed" --- {% raw %}

In order to access your organization account, you must first login to a user account that is a member of the organization team.

In order to access your organization account, follow the steps below.

Steps on Docker Cloud

  1. Log into Docker Cloud with a user account that is a member of the organization team.
  2. Click on your account name at the far right of the header, then select the organization you wish to access.
  3. Now you should have access to your organization.
  4. If you do not see the organization, an organization administrator will need to add you as a member of the organization team.

Steps on Docker Hub

  1. Log into Docker Hub with a user account that is a member of the organization team.
  2. Click Organizations at the top navigation bar.
  3. Now you should have access to your organization.
  4. If you do not see the organization, an organization administrator will need to add you as a member of the organization team.
