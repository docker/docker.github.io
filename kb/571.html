Question

Packet has returned an error, what can I do?

Answer

Here is a list of known errors thrown by Packet.net API:

  • You have reached the maximum number of projects you can create (number). Please contact help@packet.net -> Packet.net accounts have a limit in the number of projects that can be created. Delete projects in your account or contact Packet.net support to increase the limit.
  • There is an error with your Packet.net account. Please contact help@packet.net -> There is something wrong with your Packet.net account. Contact Packet.net for more details.
