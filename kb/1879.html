--- title: "What happens to my private repositories if my payment is late?" id: 1879 draftstate: inactive deleted: false source: https://success.docker.com/@api/deki/pages/1879/contents tags: - tag: "article:nontechnical" - tag: "product:cloud" - tag: "stage:reviewed" --- {% raw %}

If a payment is late, the private repository will be locked. Once a payment is made, the repository will be unlocked within minutes. If a repository is not unlocked after payment is made, please contact Support .
