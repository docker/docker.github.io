--- title: "How do I change my Docker account avatar?" id: 2099 draftstate: inactive deleted: false source: https://success.docker.com/@api/deki/pages/2099/contents tags: - tag: "article:nontechnical" - tag: "stage:reviewed" --- {% raw %}

The avatars on Docker Hub/Cloud are automatically linked to Gravatar . You can easily change your User or Organization avatar by following these steps:

  1. Set up your Gravator here: https://en.gravatar.com . (skip this step if you have already set up your Gravatar)
  2. Navigate to your Docker Hub/Cloud Account Settings to update your Gravatar email for the  Organization or User account.
  3. Fill up the Gravatar Email field with the email that you set up in Step 1.
  4. Click on Save and refresh the page.

The Organization or User account should now have an avatar displayed.
