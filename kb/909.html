The default Docker Cloud limits are as follows:

  • Containers: 300
  • Services: 150
  • Stacks: 40
  • Containers/Service: 10
  • Services/Stack:25
  • Nodes: 60
  • Node Clusters: 30
  • Nodes/Node Cluster:10
