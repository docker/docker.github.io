There are stopped docker/ucp-agent containers named ucp-reconcile on my UCP nodes. What should I do about them?

Leave them be. They are supposed to be there. UCP nodes have a stopped docker/ucp-agent container named ucp-reconcile that has the ability to execute /bin/ucp-agent recon . These containers are autogenerated by UCP and stay in a stopped state unless UCP needs to reconcile the state of the node within the cluster.
