--- title: "I have an automated build running on Docker Cloud or Docker Hub. How do I manually stop the builds?" id: 950 draftstate: inactive deleted: false source: https://success.docker.com/@api/deki/pages/950/contents tags: - tag: "article:topic" - tag: "component:hub" - tag: "expires:futurerelease" - tag: "product:cloud" - tag: "stage:reviewed" --- {% raw %}

There is currently no way to stop a running build.

On Docker Cloud, the image building process will automatically timeout and stop after 4 hours regardless of its state. On Docker Hub, builds will timeout and stop after 2 hours. Note : Builds that time out will end up in failed state.
